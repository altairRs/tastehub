Delicius soup

Tom Yam is an extremely popular dish in Thailand. It can be found at every street corner and every restaurant in the country. This dish has become famous because of its savoury and spicy taste as well as its popularity among tourists who visit Thailand. Tom yam means "hot" in Thai. Tom yam soup has many variations in Thailand â€“ including clear soup or mixed with coconut milk for added fragrance. The most common ingredients are lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, tomato, squid and prawns. Tom Yam is an incredibly flexible dish that can be eaten as a meal on its own or as an appetiser. It can also be served in a bowl with noodle or an accompaniment of a steamed jasmine rice.
